Activities

There's plenty to do when staying at our hotel. You will find here some ideas of activities for everybody. We are sure there will be one for you !

Bike Ride

That's the ideal way of discovering the region with your family. So get on your bike and let's go ! We will be happy to give you some more details and a map in case you get lost !

"Les Nuits de Sologne"

A spectacle of fireworks which takes place every year at the beginning of september to celebrate the end of the summer. If you want to learn more about the event, you can visit the following website (only in french).
